With this call, the HL-driver signals the shutdown of a D-Channel connection. This could be a response to a prior ISDN_CMD_HANGUP or caused by a remote-hangup or if the remote-station has actively rejected a call.

Parameter:
driverdriver-Id
commandISDN_STAT_DHUP
argchannel-number, locally to the driver. (starting with 0)
paraunused.
