With this call the HL-driver signals the delivery of a data-packet if the packet was sent through writebuf_skb() with the ACK flag set. This call must NOT be performed for data-packets which are sent with the ACK flag cleared.

Parameter:
driverdriver-Id
commandISDN_STAT_BSENT
argchannel-number, locally to the driver. (starting with 0)
para.lengthlength of packet.
